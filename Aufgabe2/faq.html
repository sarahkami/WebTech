FAQ

No, they work really professional, but dont make them angry - especially She Hulk (just in case).

Can I book more than one Superheroine?
Yes, you can book as many as you want. But be aware that the combination of Sailor Warriors, Powerpuff Girls and She Hulk can be difficult, as they drive her nuts.

Do you also have male Superheros?

Will I get a discount, when I book more that one Superheroine?
No. They work hard for their money, which should be honored with fair payment.
